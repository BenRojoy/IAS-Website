NANOBOTS: AN
EFFECTIVE REMEDY

 

Nanobots, which
are 50-100 nm wide, also, carry out a specific function. As a pandemic grips
the world, even doctors may forget about the threats of spreading this corona
virus while they treat their patients. So, we introduce nanites or nanobots in
order to help doctors enhance their treatment methods to cure this
disease. 

Corona virus
primarily infect the lungs and in severe cases causes death due to ARDS (Acute
Respiratory Distress Syndrome), which impact the lungs and damages the alveoli
(tiny air sacs) by thickening the thin walls of alveoli thus impairing the
transfer of oxygen to the red blood cells causing difficulty in breathing as
the body is running short of oxygen which affects other internal organs. 

Another cause of
death is due to pneumonia, another lung disease caused due to the inflammation
air sacs in one or both lungs. In this case the air sacs may fill with pus. As
this virus can spread through direct or indirect contact, doctors or other
healthcare workers would face a great threat .So, in order to avoid this risk,
we will release nanobots.
